Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our top priority. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • You must request a refund within 7 days of purchase.
  • The product or service must not have been fully consumed or delivered.
  • Proof of purchase is required.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Services already rendered or completed.
  • Custom software solutions once development has begun.
  • Any digital products that have been downloaded or accessed.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us via the Contact Page with your order details and the reason for your request.

Processing Refunds

If your refund is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment. It may take a few business days for the refund to reflect in your account.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy, feel free to reach out via our Contact Page.
